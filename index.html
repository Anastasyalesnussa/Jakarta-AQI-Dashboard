About

Hello! I’m Anastasya, an engineer and researcher who loves using data to solve real-world problems. With a background in Oil and Gas Engineering and hands-on experience in petroleum and petrochemical projects, I’ve learned how to turn complex challenges into clear, practical solutions. My work has helped me build strong analytical thinking and a passion for making things work better through data.

Now, I’m focusing on data-driven projects in energy transition and decarbonization. I’m excited about using data analytics and machine learning to create smarter, more sustainable ways to manage energy and resources. Through this portfolio, I want to share my journey of learning, exploring, and building projects that make a positive impact.
